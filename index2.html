ABOUT ME

I am José Chiseva, born on April 27, 2006. I live in the outskirts of Luanda, Angola, where I face daily challenges, but also find inspiration to grow. I am studying high school and delving into the world of programming, with a special passion for science — especially physics, mathematics and chemistry.

I am driven by curiosity, determination and the constant desire to evolve — both as a person and as a professional.
